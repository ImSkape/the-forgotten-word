Lore

As the world decends into darkness and the void terrorizes the world, 3 your children are being taken by caravan to a dwarven city for refuge.

However, after getting there, it is realized that the place has already been taken by the void and the caravan is attacked.

The only people to survive that day were those 3 kids as they barely escaped a void beast and took off into the wasteland.

Almost dying in the wasteland, the kids were rescued by a mysterious figure named Petruzzo De'Mira.

Petruzzo took the children to the City of Cinders, where they were left to grow up in an orphanage.

fow 9 years, the kids grew up in the City with the ocasional visit or letter from Petruzzo.
