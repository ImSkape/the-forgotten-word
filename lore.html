Chapter 1: The Beginning

As the world decends into darkness and the void terrorizes the world, three young children, Bosna, Herold, and Ashlyn, are being taken by a caravan to a dwarven city for refuge. However, after making the distance, it is realized that the place has already fallen to the void as the caravan is attacked. The only ones to survive that day were those three kids, nearly escaping a void beast and taking off into the wasteland. Almost freezing to death in the frigid wasteland, the children were rescued by a mysterious figure named Petruzzo De'Mira.

Petruzzo De'Mira is a complicated man. He doesn't talk much about his past, but whatever it was, it has clearly made him very jaded and distant to most. He's a member of the Seekers, a group of highly trained scouts proficient in traversing the wasteland and the complex underground cave systems. Somehow, he found himself with a group of children on the brink of death.

Feeling something for these kids, Petruzzo took them to the City of Cinders where they can grow up in an orphanage. Of course, he couldn't just leave them there without visiting from time to time. So, every once in a while, he'd make time in his plans to stop by or write a letter. Once, he even got gifts for them. But, one day, those visits stopped and the letters ran dry.

For eleven years, the kids grew up in the city, occasionally being visited Petruzzo or receiving one of his letters. But, for the rest of the time, they were on their own. They had to figure out this twisted world with nothing but each other. Each one of them did something that they resonated with them. Bosna started learning how to become a stalker, the closest thing you can become to a Seeker without having certain connections, Herold became a part of some resistances to fight the tyranny the city, and Ashlyn turned her pain into rage as she began to fight in the streets and even arenas.
